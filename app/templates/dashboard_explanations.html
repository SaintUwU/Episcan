Dashboard Guide
What Do the Percentages Mean?
Outbreak Probability: The model's confidence that a disease outbreak is occurring or will occur in the next 7 days.
  • 0-30%: Very low risk - Normal health conditions
  • 30-60%: Moderate risk - Monitor closely
  • 60-80%: High risk - Prepare response teams
  • 80-100%: Very high risk - Immediate action required
Model Accuracy
72-78% Accuracy: The model correctly predicts outbreaks 72-78% of the time. This is realistic for disease prediction - no model can be 100% accurate due to the complexity of disease spread.
Risk Levels Explained
LOW RISK:
  • Normal health discussions
  • Routine health updates
  • No concerning patterns detected
MEDIUM RISK:
  • Increased health-related activity
  • Some concerning keywords detected
  • Monitor for escalation
HIGH RISK:
  • Multiple outbreak indicators
  • Geographic concentration of cases
  • Urgent action recommended
How the Model Works
News Analysis

Monitors health news from Kenyan media for outbreak reports

Social Media

Analyzes Twitter posts for health discussions and symptoms

Search Trends

Tracks Google searches for health-related terms

Geographic Focus

The system monitors all 47 Kenyan counties and can identify specific high-risk areas. When multiple sources mention the same county with health concerns, the risk level increases.
